About Me

I design contract-theoretic and reinforcement learning frameworks for intelligent transportation systems, with a focus on Automated Valet Parking & Charging (AVPC), multi-agent systems, and large language models for network and service management. My broader research interests include 5G/6G networks, UAV-assisted IoT, and blockchain for resource trading.

Research Keywords: AVPC · Multi-Agent RL · Contract Theory · 5G/6G · Vehicular Networks · UAVs · LLMs
