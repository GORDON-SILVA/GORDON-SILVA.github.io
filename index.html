About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Communications and Networking at XJTLU. I design contract-theoretic and multi-agent reinforcement learning frameworks for shared Automated Valet Parking & Charging (AVPC) and intelligent transportation systems, and explore LLM-assisted scheduling/planning for network and service management. My broader interests include 5G/6G networks, UAV-assisted IoT, and blockchain-enabled resource trading.

Keywords: AVPC · Multi-Agent RL · Hybrid Action Spaces · Contract Theory · 5G/6G · Vehicular Networks · UAVs · LLMs
